Privacy Policy

Daily Wonder Lab — Last updated: July 5, 2026

Daily Wonder Lab ("we", "our") produces short animated science-education videos and publishes them to our own social media channels, including our own TikTok account, using an internal publishing application ("the App") built on the TikTok Content Posting API.

Who uses the App

The App is an internal tool. It is used only by the operator of Daily Wonder Lab to post our own original videos to our own TikTok account. It is not offered to the public, has no user registration, and does not provide any service to third parties.

What data the App handles

The App handles only the following data, all relating to our own account:

  • OAuth access and refresh tokens issued by TikTok when we authorize the App on our own account. These are stored locally on our own computer and are never shared with anyone.
  • Basic account information (display name and open ID) returned by TikTok, used only to confirm the App is connected to the correct account.
  • The videos and captions we upload, which are our own original content.

What the App does not do

  • It does not collect, store, or process any personal data from TikTok users, viewers, followers, or any other third party.
  • It does not use cookies, analytics, tracking, or advertising technologies.
  • It does not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

Data retention and deletion

OAuth tokens are kept locally only for as long as the App is in use and can be revoked at any time from TikTok's app-permission settings, which immediately invalidates them. No other data is retained.

Changes to this policy

If the App's behavior ever changes in a way that affects this policy, we will update this page and revise the date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to suficrust@gmail.com.
